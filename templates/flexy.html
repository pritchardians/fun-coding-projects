Flex Display

In a flexbox, items line horizontally. By adding a wrap flag, they flow on to the next line. when they run out of space. I don't specify specific width for the items, so they flow responsively. On my screen, the last item is on a row by itself and takes uo the whole container width, but this will vary by screen!

While you may believe there is margin between the cells, we can use a propo=erty called 'gap' on the container. This adds a gap between items.

Look at cell 6. Is it a bit wider than its fellow cells? This is by setting a flex-grow property
